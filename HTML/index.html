DOTO 2

Doto 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game developed and published by Valve.

The game is a sequel to Defense of the Obsoletes (DotO), which was a community-created mod for Blizzard Entertainment's Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos.

Doto 2 is played in matches between two teams of five players, with each team occupying and defending their own separate base on the map.

Each of the ten players independently controls a powerful character, known as a "hero", who all have unique abilities and differing styles of play.

During a match players collect experience points and items for their heroes to successfully defeat the opposing team's heroes in player versus player combat.

A team wins by being the first to destroy the other team's "Obsoletes", a large structure located within their base.
